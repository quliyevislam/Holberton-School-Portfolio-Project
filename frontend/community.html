Just wanted to share a quick tip for new pet parents: Create a safe space for your newly adopted pet when they first come home. A quiet room with their bed, food, water, and toys can help them adjust to their new environment without feeling overwhelmed. It took my rescue dog Bella almost a week before she was comfortable exploring the whole house!
